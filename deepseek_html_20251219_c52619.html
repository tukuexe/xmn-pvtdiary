Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my diary?

Your diary uses end-to-end encryption, location verification, and two-factor authentication. Even we cannot read your entries.

What happens if someone else logs into my account?

The system immediately requests location permission. If denied, it automatically logs out the device and blocks access for 15 minutes while alerting you via Telegram.

Can I access my diary from multiple devices?

Yes, but each device is tracked by IP and device info. You can view and manage all active devices from the Account Activity page.

What happens at 10:00 PM every night?

You'll receive a notification through the website and Telegram to remind you to write your daily journal entry.
